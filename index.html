The lenses

    Methodology & caveats

    Survivorship bias: companies that went bankrupt or were delisted can drop out of a "currently listed" universe, so all-time growth tables flatter the survivors. Era bias: raw total growth always favours the oldest stocks — that's why annual growth (CAGR) and per-decade views matter. All prices are split- and dividend-adjusted. Valuation lenses are screens built from public multiples, not investment advice.
